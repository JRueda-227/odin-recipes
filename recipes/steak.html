Steak Recipe

Description

To cook the perfect steak is simple.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Neutral Tasting Oil
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pat dry steak and season generously with salt.
  2. Heat up cast iron skillet with oil until hot.
  3. Cook steak 2-3 minutes each side until an internal temperature of 135 degrees.
  4. After flipping once, place butter, garlic, and rosemary in skillet and use a spoon to baste the steak with the butter.
  5. Remove steak from skillet and let rest for 10 minutes.
