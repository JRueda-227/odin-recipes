Marinated Flank Steak

A great flank steak marinade like this one is important if you want a tender, juicy, flavorful steak. Make sure you marinate your flank steak for at least 5 hours for best results or longer if you have time. This wonderful quick and easy recipe also works great when the steak is sliced and used for fajitas.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • vegetable oil
  • red wine
  • lemon juice
  • soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper

Steak

  • flank steak

Steps

  1. mix marinade ingredients and cover steak
  2. ONLY! cook steak medium rare
