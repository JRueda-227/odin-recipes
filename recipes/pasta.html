Aglio e Olio Recipe

Description

Beauty in simplicity.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. While heating up oil in skillet place garlic.
  3. When garlic becomes toasted add red pepper flakes and toss in pasta with a splash of pasta water.
  4. Turn off heat, mix in parsley and serve.
