Burge Recipe

Description

A Basic yet savory lunch.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • American Cheese
  • Butter
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Smash a 4oz beef patty on a very hot iron skillet.
  2. Flip after 2 minutes and place cheese.
  3. Remove patty and place buns to toast for 30 seconds.
  4. Spread mustard on buns and stack with the patty and cheese.
